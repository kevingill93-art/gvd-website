Data Deletion Request

Last updated: May 2026

You have the right to request deletion of any personal information we hold about you.

What We May Hold

We may hold your email address (if you subscribed), any messages you've sent us, and basic analytics. See our Privacy Policy.

How to Request Deletion

Email mayhem@godvsthedevil.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request". Send it from the email associated with your data so we can locate and remove your information.

Unsubscribing from Email

To stop receiving emails, every message includes a one-click unsubscribe link. See our Email & Data Use page.

What Happens Next

Once verified, we will delete the information we hold about you, except anything we must retain by law, and confirm when done.

Data From Connected Platforms

Deleting your data with us does not delete data held by a social platform — manage that through the platform directly.
