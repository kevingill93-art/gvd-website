Email & Data Use

Last updated: May 2026

This page explains exactly what happens when you give us your email address — what we send, how we store it, and how you can leave at any time. We keep this simple on purpose.

What You're Signing Up For

When you subscribe, you're asking to receive updates about God vs the Devil — things like new episodes, comics, memes, behind-the-scenes news, and the occasional announcement. That's it.

How Often We Email

We email when we actually have something worth your time. We will not flood your inbox.

How to Unsubscribe

Every email we send includes a one-click unsubscribe link at the bottom. Click it once and you're removed automatically — no reply needed, no waiting, no hoops. You can also email mayhem@godvsthedevil.com and ask to be removed, and we'll honor it. We will never require you to contact us by hand to opt out.

What We Do With Your Email Address

  • We use it only to send the updates you signed up for.
  • We store it securely with a reputable email service provider.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your email address to anyone.
  • We may look at anonymized, aggregated data (for example, how many people opened an update) to improve what we send.

Demographic & Audience Information

To understand who enjoys the project and to plan future content, we may collect general, aggregated audience information — such as broad location or interests — through our email and analytics tools. This is used in aggregate to make better content, not to single you out.

Keeping Our List Clean

If you stop opening our emails for a long time, we may remove you to keep our list healthy. You're always welcome back.

Compliance

We follow applicable email and privacy laws, including the U.S. CAN-SPAM Act, and we honor unsubscribe requests promptly. For how we handle your information more broadly, see our Privacy Policy.

Questions

Reach us anytime at mayhem@godvsthedevil.com.
