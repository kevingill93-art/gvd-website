The Project

Heaven and Hell have been at it since the beginning of time. Most of the fighting? Not actually about us. It's about a parking spot, a group chat, and a fish who won't stop overexplaining things.

It's therapy. We just animated it.

God vs the Devil is an original animated comedy from Dr. K — the universe's oldest feud, drawn loud, voiced louder, and weirdly honest about the stuff most shows won't touch. Anxiety, dads, group projects, mortality, the dentist. You know. The hits.

We punch up. Never down. The mortals are us. The monsters are bosses, bullies, and our own brains at 3am. Everything lives here first — uncut, no notes, no algorithm — and stays here, no matter what the platforms do tomorrow.
