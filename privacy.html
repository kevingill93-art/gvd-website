This Site is operated from the State of Florida, United States. You can reach us at any time at mayhem@godvsthedevil.com.

We do not sell your personal information.

If you subscribe, we will email you updates about the project. Every email includes a one-click unsubscribe link, and you may opt out at any time — see our Email & Data Use page for full details. We will never make you contact us by hand to unsubscribe.

The Site and its content are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will delete it.

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, and you may unsubscribe from emails at any time. To make a request, email mayhem@godvsthedevil.com.
