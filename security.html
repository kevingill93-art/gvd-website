Security Policy

Last updated: May 2026

We take the security of this Site and of any information you share with us seriously. This page explains the measures we use and how to report a concern.

How the Site Is Protected

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS). All traffic to and from godvsthedevil.com is encrypted with SSL/TLS, shown by the padlock in your browser.
  • Secure hosting infrastructure. The Site is served from a modern content delivery network with built-in protection against common attacks, including distributed denial-of-service (DDoS) attempts.
  • Reduced attack surface. The Site is built to minimize vulnerabilities and does not expose unnecessary administrative interfaces to the public.

How Your Data Is Handled

  • We collect only the information we need, as described in our Privacy Policy.
  • Email addresses and communications are processed through reputable third-party providers that maintain their own security standards.
  • Payment information (when we add a store in the future) will be handled by established, PCI-compliant payment processors — we will not store full payment card details ourselves.

Your Role in Security

Please help keep your information safe: use a strong, unique password for any accounts you create with us in the future, be cautious of emails claiming to be from us that ask for sensitive information, and verify that links genuinely point to godvsthedevil.com. We will never ask for your password by email.

Reporting a Vulnerability or Concern

If you believe you have found a security vulnerability, or you have a concern about how the Site or your data is handled, please contact us at mayhem@godvsthedevil.com. We appreciate responsible disclosure and will respond as quickly as we reasonably can.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Security Policy as our Site and practices evolve. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.
